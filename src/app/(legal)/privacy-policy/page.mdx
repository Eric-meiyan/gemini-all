# Privacy Policy for Gemini CLI Hub

## Introduction

Welcome to Gemini CLI Hub, the **comprehensive resource center** designed to provide you with the latest news, expert blogs, and essential tools for Gemini CLI development. At Gemini CLI Hub, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using Gemini CLI Hub:

1. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account, provide personalized content recommendations, and offer customer support.

2. **Usage Details**

   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use Gemini CLI Hub, including articles read, tools accessed, search queries, and usage frequency.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement, improve our content recommendations, and enhance our services.

3. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access Gemini CLI Hub, such as device type, operating system, browser type, and screen resolution.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices and ensure compatibility across platforms.

4. **Cookies**

   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences, reading history, and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our services, remember your preferences, and personalize your content experience.

5. **Newsletter and Communication Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information regarding your subscription preferences, email interactions, and communication history.
   - **Purpose**: To send you relevant updates, newsletters, and information about new content that matches your interests.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. The data we collect is stored securely on our servers, and we implement a variety of security measures including encryption, secure data transmission protocols, and access controls to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
- To provide services through trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our website, content delivery, or conducting our business, subject to strict confidentiality agreements.
- To share anonymized usage statistics with content creators and tool developers to help improve the Gemini CLI ecosystem.

## Content and User Contributions

When you contribute content, submit feedback, or participate in our community discussions:

- We may use your contributions to improve our services and help other users.
- Your public contributions (such as comments or reviews) may be visible to other users.
- We respect your intellectual property rights and expect you to respect others' rights as well.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this privacy policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

You have the right to:

- Opt out of marketing communications
- Request information about how your data is used
- Lodge a complaint with relevant data protection authorities
